3D Arc Picker: How To Use

This interactive 3D environment allows you to view and **select a curved path (Arc)** using specialized GPU picking technology. Follow the instructions below to navigate and interact with the scene.

Scene Interaction (Orbit Controls)

1.
Rotate View: Click and hold the **Left Mouse Button (LMB)** anywhere on the 3D Canvas and drag your mouse.
2.
Zoom In/Out: Use your **Scroll Wheel**.
3.
Pan View (Move Camera): Click and hold the **Right Mouse Button (RMB)** (or the scroll wheel) and drag to shift the scene position.

Object Selection (GPU Picking)

1.
Select the Arc: Simply move your cursor (hover) over the thick, curved path. You do not need to click.
2.
Visual Feedback: When the arc is successfully detected by the GPU Picker, its color will change to a bright **Sunset Orange** for immediate feedback.
3.
Status: Check the **Top-Left Status Panel** for confirmation that the "Arc is Selected."
If you encounter issues, please check your browser console for Three.js errors.
